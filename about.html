Our Core Values

Core Values

01

Integrity Above All

Cannot be sold, bought, or pressured into compromising the standard. Our independence is our most valuable asset — and it's legally protected.

02

Committed to Human Creativity

The work of human creators is the foundation of the AI economy, not an obstacle. Their contribution deserves acknowledgement, protection, and fair compensation.

03

Devoted to AI Innovation

The goal is progress for both sides, not slowing AI down. A well-licensed AI ecosystem is a stronger, more defensible one.

04

Lead with Kindness and Partnership

Contentious but value-positive problems can be solved collaboratively — and we're building the infrastructure to prove it.

05

Credibility and Accuracy are Paramount

Every claim is backed by research and methodology. The standard is only as strong as the science behind it.
