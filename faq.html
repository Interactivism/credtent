Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Credtent, our services, and how we empower creators in the AI era.

For Creators

Credtent allows you to register your creative works and either opt-out of AI training data usage entirely, or license your work for fair compensation. When you opt-out, we send comprehensive opt-out requests to all major AI companies and monitor for compliance. We also provide violation reporting and regular audits to ensure your preferences are respected.

Compensation varies depending on content type, volume, market demand, and the specific licensing terms you set. Credtent uses a science-driven content valuation approach to establish fair market prices. Payments are distributed quarterly, minus our 15% platform fee and any applicable transaction costs. Premium plan members can also set their own pricing.

Credtent supports a wide range of creative works including stories, blog posts, articles, photos, illustrations, art, films, social media content, graphic designs, video games, music, and more. If you created it and it has been or could be published online, you can register it. We're continuously expanding our supported content types.

The opt-out process is simple: create a free account, register your works and website, and declare your preferences. Credtent will then submit comprehensive opt-out requests to all major AI companies on your behalf and track the compliance status. The basic opt-out is completely free and always will be.

No. Basic opt-out protection is free, forever. We believe every creator deserves the right to say no to AI training, regardless of their resources. Premium features like detailed compliance reporting, content valuation, and custom pricing controls are available on paid plans.

For AI Companies

Credtent provides a transparent, consensual licensing framework with full monitoring tools. AI companies can access a verified registry of consented content, obtain proper licenses with clear terms, and receive corpus disclosure compliance documentation. We serve as a neutral third party to facilitate fair agreements between AI companies and content creators.

Content pricing uses a science-driven approach that considers content type, quality, uniqueness, market demand, and current fair market value for similar works. Creators can accept Credtent's standard pricing or, on Premium plans, set their own rates. AI companies can trust that pricing reflects genuine fair-market value, reducing legal and reputational risk.

AI companies have access to detailed usage and compliance transparency reports, corpus disclosure documentation, certified AI transparency audits, and ongoing monitoring. These tools help demonstrate responsible AI development to regulators, investors, and the public.

For Organizations & Groups

Yes. Group licensing is available for guilds, unions, schools, media organizations, and communities. Group plans include reduced pricing, an advisory board seat, unified member management, and dedicated account support. Pricing is customized based on group size and specific needs.

Creator Advisory Board members provide input on platform policies, pricing frameworks, and industry standards. This gives organizations a voice in how Credtent develops its services and how AI licensing norms are shaped. It's a meaningful opportunity to influence the future of creator rights in the AI age.
