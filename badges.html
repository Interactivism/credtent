Four Badge Types

Choose Your Creative Origin

Select the badge that accurately represents how your work was created. Transparent, standardized, and recognized across the industry.

Human-Composed Creation

The creative work was human-created, possibly using insightful AI as a research or reference tool — but the creative output, decisions, and voice are entirely yours.

When to use this badge

Your work is entirely human-created. You may have used AI as a dictionary or search tool, but not as a generative creative partner.

AI-Assisted Creation

Generative AI tools have been used to refine, edit, suggest, or enhance the work of a human creator. The original creative vision and direction remains human.

When to use this badge

You used AI tools to generate drafts, suggest edits, create reference imagery, or enhance your work — but the guiding creative hand is yours.

AI-Composed Creation

The creative work was primarily created by a generative AI. A human directed the process and reviewed the output, but the core creative generation is machine-driven.

When to use this badge

A generative AI system produced the majority of the content. You provided prompts, direction, and curation, but the AI did the primary creative work.

Legacy Creation

The creative work was created before November 2022, without generative AI tools — predating the modern generative AI era. This badge protects pre-AI creative work.

When to use this badge

Your work was created before the launch of ChatGPT (November 2022) and therefore could not have involved modern generative AI tools.
